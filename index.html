LEGACY

This Week's Scriptures

  • 2 Timothy 1:3-5
  • 2 Timothy 3:14-15

Pointers

  • Faith always produces and reproduces.
  • Just as it is important to have biological parents, it is important to have spiritual parents.
  • God puts people in our lives purposely- because He sees where we are going.
  • God will never send us to an environment where He has not prepared us to be.
  • God positions us every season for something He has for us.
  • Paul speaks of the Lois and Eunice and how God had placed them purposely that Timothy could benefit from the faith that they had.
  • Paul implies that it is by these women's (Lois' and Eunice's) faith and prayer that Timothy is standing where he was.
  • By extension, God has placed the mothers in our lives for where we are going.
  • To the women, your faith will follow you throughout generations.
  • There is no better gift we can pass on than the Faith and trust in the ability of God
  • We will never know whether we have influenced other people until we have influenced them.
  • God has wired women to be problem solvers.
  • We have to honor the mother figures, the women of faith in our lives because they speak life into us; they position us for our greatness.
  • We may not have a biological mother around, but God will always position a mother figure to fill the gap.
  • Paul in writing to Timothy wants him not to forget the instructions he got.
  • Any person/child that values the instructtions of people who have been there gain more than what money and time cannot buy.
  • God has wired all of us to be people of influence.
