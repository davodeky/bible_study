LEGACY

This Week's Scriptures

  • 2 Timothy 1:3-5
  • 2 Timothy 3:14-15

Pointers

  • Faith always produces and reproduces.
  • Just as it is important to have biological parents, it is important to have spiritual parents.
  • God puts people in our lives purposely- because He sees where we are going.
  • God will never send us to an environment where He has not prepared us to be.
  • God positions us every season for something He has for us.
  • Paul speaks of the Lois and Eunice and how God had placed them purposely that Timothy could benefit from the faith that they had.
  • Paul implies that it is by these women's (Lois' and Eunice's) faith and prayer that Timothy is standing where he was.
  • By extension, God has placed the mothers in our lives for where we are going.
